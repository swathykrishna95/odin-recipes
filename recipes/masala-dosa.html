Masala Dosa Recipe

Description

The famous Dosa needs no introduction. It is popular all over South India, and North as well. This is a variation that will have some masala paste in the middle of the dosa.

  • Idli Rice
  • Urad Dal, Methi seeds
  • Oil
  • Water, Salt
  • Potato
  • Channa Dal
  • Green chillies, Ginger, Coriander leaves
  • Mustard seeds, Curry leaves
  • Hing, Turmeric powder

Steps

  1. Blend the rice and dal, methi mixture in a mixi.
  2. Ensure that the batter is smooth and consistent.
  3. Leave the batter overnight in a closed vessel for fermenting.
  4. Boil the potatoes till they are soft.
  5. Soak the channa dal in hot water for half an hour.
  6. Peel and chop the potatoes.
  7. Chop the green chillies, ginger and coriander leaves.
  8. Heat the oil in a pan.
  9. Add mustard seeds and let them crackle.
  10. Add the channa dal and saute for a few minutes until they are golden brown.
  11. Add the chopped green chillies, curry leaves and ginger.
  12. Add turmeric powder and hing to the pan.
  13. Add water and stir till the mixture gains a little thickness.
  14. Add the sliced potatoes. Mix very well.
  15. Add salt for taste.
  16. Cook for 4-5 minutes, then turn off the heat and add the coriander leaves.
  17. Heat a dosa pan and make the dosas. Make sure that they are large and thin.
  18. When both sides are cooked, turn over and spread the potato filling, and fold the dosa.
